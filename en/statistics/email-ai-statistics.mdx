The **Statistics → Email AI** page shows you how your email AI is being used. This page explains what each number means.

<Info>
Use the **date range** in the top right (default: last month). All metrics always refer to the selected period.
</Info>

## Basic KPIs

| Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Emails** | Number of emails processed by the AI in the period. |
| **Booking links** | Number of booking links included in the answers. |
| **Share of booking links** | Share of emails that contained a booking link. |
| **Generated words** | Total number of words written by the AI. |
| **Offered nights** | Sum of the nights offered in the answers. |
| **Emails over time** | Development of processed emails over the period. |
| **Language share** | Distribution of emails by language. |

<Note>
"Offered nights" are the nights proposed in the email answers – not necessarily nights that were actually booked.
</Note>

## Example

How to read the numbers together:

- **500 emails**, of which **75 with a booking link** → share of booking links = **15%**.
- **Language share** shows e.g. German 70%, English 25%, French 5% – so you can see which languages your guests write in.
