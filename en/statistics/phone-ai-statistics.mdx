The **Statistics → Phone AI** page shows you how your AI phone assistant is being used. This page explains what each number means.

<Info>
Use the **date range** in the top right (default: last month). All metrics always refer to the selected period.
</Info>

## Basic KPIs

| Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Calls** | Total number of calls to the Phone AI. |
| **Conversations** | Calls that turned into a real conversation (no immediate hang-up). |
| **Conversation conversion** | Share of calls that became a conversation. |
| **Redirects** | Share of conversations that were forwarded to a human. |
| **Distribution callers** | Split of callers by landline, mobile and anonymous. |
| **Minutes** | Sum of all conversation minutes. |
| **Conversation length (min)** | Average length of a conversation. |
| **Conversations (trend)** | Development of conversations over the period. |
| **Minutes per day** | Conversation minutes per day (per week for long ranges). |

<Note>
"Anonymous" means the caller's number was withheld or could not be identified. A call without a real conversation (caller hangs up immediately) counts towards **Calls** but not **Conversations**.
</Note>

## Booking KPIs

| Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Booking links** | Number of booking links sent during the call via SMS or WhatsApp. |
| **WhatsApp** | Share of booking links sent via WhatsApp (only shown if WhatsApp is active). |
| **Booking EUR** | Revenue from bookings by callers (only shown if booking revenue is tracked). |

<Info>
**Booking EUR** only appears if booking revenue is tracked for your hotel – via the DialogShift tracking pixel in your booking engine or via an integration (e.g. HNS).
</Info>

## Conversation content

| Chart | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Intents** | Distribution of callers' main requests (e.g. requested human, booking, spa / wellness, hotel information). |
| **Sentiments** | Mood of the conversations: positive, neutral or negative. |

<Note>
Intents and sentiments are detected automatically by the AI for each conversation.
</Note>

## Example

How to read the numbers together:

- **2437 calls**, of which **2032 conversations** → conversation conversion = **83%**: 83% of calls turned into a real conversation.
- **2336 minutes** across 2032 conversations → conversation length = **1.15 min** on average.
- **Redirects 66%**: two out of three conversations were forwarded to a human.
- **Distribution callers:** mobile 66%, landline 32%, anonymous 3%.
