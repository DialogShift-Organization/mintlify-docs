Hotels use the WhatsApp Business Platform to communicate with guests via WhatsApp. DialogShift uses this platform to run the chatbot and live chat on this channel.

**Product used:** [WhatsApp Business Platform](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/business-platform)

## How Guests Reach the Hotel via WhatsApp

- **QR code on the website** – guests scan it to open a chat
- **QR code on-site** – placed in the hotel or on print materials
- **Click-to-chat link** – for WhatsApp Web users
- **Click-to-WhatsApp ads** – Facebook or Instagram ads that open a WhatsApp chat

<Note>
WhatsApp does not allow businesses to send the first message. The guest must always initiate contact. When scanning a QR code through DialogShift, the opening message is pre-filled in the input field.
</Note>

## Available Chat Functions

Within the 24-hour window (see below):

- Text messages
- Quick reply buttons (max. 3 per message; labels are cut off after 20 characters)
- Images and videos (send and receive)
- Voice messages (receive only – hotels cannot send these)
- Location messages
- List messages for structured selections

<Note>
WhatsApp does not support native carousels. DialogShift automatically converts carousels into simplified text messages with up to 3 buttons each.
</Note>

## 24-Hour Rule

After a guest contacts the hotel, the hotel can chat freely for the next 24 hours. After this window closes, only pre-approved template messages can be sent.

Template categories:
- **Utility** – service updates, booking confirmations
- **Authentication** – verifications, login codes
- **Marketing** – offers, newsletters (opt-out required)

## Requirements

- A Facebook Business Manager account
- A dedicated phone number that can receive calls (landline or mobile)
- Meta Business Verification (can be completed later in the process)

**One number for multiple hotels**

Using a single WhatsApp number across multiple hotels is possible if the displayed business name is a registered trademark, there is a verifiable connection to the operator, and a brand website exists.

## Migrating an Existing WhatsApp Number

If the hotel currently uses a regular WhatsApp number, it can be migrated to the Business Platform. What is lost in the migration:

- All previous chats and contacts
- The existing WhatsApp app on that number

After migration, the number works as before for guests – they still see the hotel as a regular contact and can message again as usual.

## Messaging Limits and Quality

Initial limits per 24-hour period:

- Unverified business: 250 unique contacts
- Verified business: 1,000 unique contacts
- Limits can increase automatically after 7 days of good standing

WhatsApp monitors quality metrics including blocking rate, user reports, response times, and template performance. A poor quality rating can reduce limits or result in account suspension.

## Costs

Billing is per 24-hour conversation, not per message:

- **Service conversations** (guest-initiated) – lower rate
- **Business-initiated conversations** – higher rate; three categories: Utility, Authentication, Marketing

The first 1,000 service conversations per month are free. Prices vary by the recipient's country.

## Onboarding

Activating WhatsApp requires an application through DialogShift as an authorized Meta service provider. DialogShift coordinates the setup process including number verification and Meta certification.
