description: "How to activate the integration with Hotel Net Solutions to enable AI bookings and track revenue from chat and phone in DialogShift"
---

The integration with Hotel Net Solutions (HNS) connects DialogShift to your OnePageBooking engine. Chat AI and Phone AI retrieve real-time availability, prices and rates, enable bookings, and record the revenue generated through them in the DialogShift app.

The connection is used by:

- **Availability Agent** – availability and price information in chat and on the phone.
- **Full Booking Agent** – complete booking in chat and on the phone.

## Prerequisites

- Your hotel uses OnePageBooking from HNS.
- The integration is commissioned with HNS (paid service at HNS).
- Your DialogShift Client ID (in the app under **Settings → Integration**).

## Activate the integration

1. Commission the DialogShift integration with HNS.
2. HNS sends the API credentials to you or directly to DialogShift.
3. The credentials are entered on the HNS integration page in the DialogShift app.
4. DialogShift sends your Client ID to HNS.

The integration is then active.

## Configuration

On the HNS integration page in the DialogShift app you define which room categories and rates the booking agents may sell, and add the corresponding images.
