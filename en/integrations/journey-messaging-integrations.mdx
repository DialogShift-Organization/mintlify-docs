---
title: "Journey Messaging Integrations"
description: "Which PMS integrations DialogShift uses for automated Journey Messaging flows."
---

Journey Messaging integrations send booking and guest data to DialogShift. DialogShift uses that data to create journeys and events that trigger automated WhatsApp, SMS or email messages.

| System | Journey Messaging status | Updates | Activation | Details |
|:-------|:-------------------------|:--------|:-----------|:--------|
| apaleo | Supported | Webhooks, near real-time | Hotel connects apaleo in the DialogShift app | [apaleo Integration](/en/integrations/apaleo-integration) |
| ASA | Supported, Journey Messaging only | Polling, about every 15 minutes | Customer Success activates the PMS connection | [ASA Integration](/en/integrations/asa-integration) |
| Casablanca | Closed Beta Rollout | PMS webhooks | Customer Success activates the PMS connection | [Casablanca Integration](/en/integrations/casablanca-integration) |
| MEWS | Supported | Webhooks, usually shortly after the PMS update | MEWS Marketplace + DialogShift app | [MEWS Integration](/en/integrations/mews-booking-agent-go-live) |
| Oracle OHIP | Closed Beta Rollout | Polling, about every 15 minutes | Customer Success activates the PMS connection | [Oracle OHIP Integration](/en/integrations/oracle-ohip-integration) |
| Protel / Planet | Closed Beta Rollout | Polling, about every 15 minutes | DialogShift app + Customer Success context for PMS | [Protel / Planet Integration](/en/integrations/protel-planet-integration) |

These booking engine integrations do not currently provide Journey Messaging data: HNS, HotelPartner, Bookolo, DIRS21, Ennismore, Samera and Workmatrix.

For flow, template and channel basics, see the [Journey Messaging overview](/en/journey-messaging/overview).
