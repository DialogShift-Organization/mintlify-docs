We're excited to invite you to a training session for our DialogShift app. Here you'll learn everything you need to know to effectively use your hotel AI.

## Training Details

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Duration" icon="clock">
    45 minutes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Format" icon="video">
    Online training via Zoom (can be recorded)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to Expect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Comprehensive Introduction">
    A complete introduction to all features of the DialogShift app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practical Examples">
    Concrete use cases to optimally integrate the app into your daily workflow
  </Step>
  <Step title="Q&A Session">
    Time for your questions to clarify any uncertainties
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
After the training, you'll receive an email with the most important information so you can access it anytime.
</Info>

## Next Steps

To schedule a training session with your Customer Success Manager:

1. Ensure you have completed all onboarding tasks
2. Send an email to [support@dialogshift.com](mailto:support@dialogshift.com)
3. Include your preferred time slots

<Note>
We look forward to welcoming you to our Zoom training session and helping you get the most out of our application!
</Note>
