## What is the Client ID?

The **Client ID** uniquely identifies your account/hotel in DialogShift and is used for **integrations with partner companies**.

## Where to find the Client ID

You can find the Client ID directly in the **URL** of the DialogShift App.

<img
  src="/images/client-id-url.png"
  alt="Client ID in the DialogShift App URL"
/>

It always:

- starts with **`pro`**
- is followed by **4 letters or numbers**

### Example

If your URL looks like this:

`https://app.dialogshift.com/pro1c75/hotel/settings/general`

Then the **Client ID** is:

`pro1c75`

## How it’s used

Provide the Client ID to a partner company (or DialogShift Support) whenever an integration needs to be connected to the correct DialogShift client.
